Subjective Questions

Q1. Find the equation of the circle with center $(-2, 3)$ and radius 4.

Show Answer
Solution: $(x - (-2))^2 + (y - 3)^2 = 4^2$.
$(x+2)^2 + (y-3)^2 = 16 \implies x^2+y^2+4x-6y-3=0$.

Q2. Find the focus and directrix of the parabola $x^2 = -16y$.

Show Answer
Solution: Form $x^2 = -4ay \implies 4a=16 \implies a=4$.
Downward parabola. Focus $(0, -4)$. Directrix $y=4$.

Q3. Find the eccentricity of the ellipse $9x^2 + 4y^2 = 36$.

Show Answer
Solution: Divide by 36: $\frac{x^2}{4} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1$.
$a^2=9$ (under y), $b^2=4$. Vertical ellipse.
$c = \sqrt{9-4} = \sqrt{5}$. $e = c/a = \sqrt{5}/3$.

Q4. Find the coordinates of foci for hyperbola $y^2 - 16x^2 = 16$.

Show Answer
Solution: $\frac{y^2}{16} - \frac{x^2}{1} = 1$. Vertical hyperbola.
$a^2=16, b^2=1$. $c^2 = 16+1=17 \implies c=\sqrt{17}$.
Foci $(0, \pm \sqrt{17})$.
