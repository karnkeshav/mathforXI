Subjective Questions

Q1. Solve $3x - 7 > 5x - 1$ for $x \in \mathbb{R}$.

Show Answer
Solution: $3x - 5x > -1 + 7$
$-2x > 6$
Divide by -2 (flip sign): $x < -3$.
Interval: $(-\infty, -3)$.

Q2. Solve $-12 < 4 - \frac{3x}{-5} \le 2$.

Show Answer
Solution: Subtract 4: $-16 < \frac{3x}{5} \le -2$.
Multiply by 5/3: $\frac{-80}{3} < x \le \frac{-10}{3}$.

Q3. Ravi obtained 70 and 75 marks in first two unit tests. Find the minimum marks he should get in the third test to have an average of at least 60 marks.

Show Answer
Solution: Let $x$ be marks. $\frac{70+75+x}{3} \ge 60$.
$145+x \ge 180 \implies x \ge 35$.

Q4. Draw the graph of $x+y \le 5$.

Show Answer
Solution: Line $x+y=5$ passing through $(5,0), (0,5)$. Solid line.
Test $(0,0): 0 \le 5$ (True). Shade region towards origin.
