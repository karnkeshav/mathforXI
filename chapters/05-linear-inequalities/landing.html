Linear Inequalities

The mathematics of constraints. Regions, ranges, and optimization in the real world.

Why This Chapter Matters

In real life, things are rarely "exactly equal". You have a budget limit. A car has a maximum speed. A bridge has a load capacity.

Equations tell us what IS. Inequalities tell us what CAN BE. They are the language of constraints and optimization, forming the basis of Linear Programming in Class XII and operations research in business.

This chapter answers:
“How do we find the best solution within given limits?”

What You Will Learn

Algebraic Solutions

Solving inequalities in one variable and representing them on a number line.

Graphical Solutions

Shading regions in a 2D plane that satisfy given conditions.

Systems of Inequalities

Finding the common solution region for multiple constraints.

Applications

Solving word problems involving ranges and limits.
