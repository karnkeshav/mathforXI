Subjective Questions

Q1. Express $(5-3i)^3$ in the form $a+ib$.

Show Answer
Solution: $(5-3i)^3 = 5^3 - 3(5)^2(3i) + 3(5)(3i)^2 - (3i)^3$
$= 125 - 225i + 15(-9) - 27(-i)$
$= 125 - 225i - 135 + 27i = -10 - 198i$.

Q2. Find the multiplicative inverse of $2-3i$.

Show Answer
Solution: Inverse $z^{-1} = \frac{\bar{z}}{|z|^2} = \frac{2+3i}{2^2+(-3)^2} = \frac{2+3i}{4+9} = \frac{2}{13} + i\frac{3}{13}$.

Q3. Convert $-1-i$ into polar form.

Show Answer
Solution: $r = \sqrt{(-1)^2+(-1)^2} = \sqrt{2}$.
$\tan \alpha = |-1/-1| = 1 \implies \alpha = \pi/4$.
Point in III Quadrant, so $\theta = -(\pi-\pi/4) = -3\pi/4$.
Polar form: $\sqrt{2}(\cos(-3\pi/4) + i\sin(-3\pi/4))$.

Q4. Solve $x^2 + 3x + 9 = 0$.

Show Answer
Solution: $D = 3^2 - 4(1)(9) = 9 - 36 = -27$.
$x = \frac{-3 \pm \sqrt{-27}}{2} = \frac{-3 \pm 3\sqrt{3}i}{2}$.
