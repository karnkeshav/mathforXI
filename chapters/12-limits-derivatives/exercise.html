Subjective Questions

Q1. Evaluate the limit: $$\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 1}{x - 1}$$

Show Answer
Solution: $$\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x-1)(x+1)}{x-1} = \lim_{x \to 1} (x+1) = 1+1 = 2$$

Q2. Find the derivative of $f(x) = x^2$ using First Principles.

Show Answer
Solution: $$f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(x+h)^2 - x^2}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{x^2+2xh+h^2-x^2}{h}$$ $$= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{h(2x+h)}{h} = 2x$$

Q4. Find the derivative of $f(x) = x^3$ using first principles.

Show Answer
Solution: $$\lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(x+h)^3 - x^3}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{x^3+3x^2h+3xh^2+h^3-x^3}{h}$$ $$= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{h(3x^2+3xh+h^2)}{h} = 3x^2$$
