Why Calculus Exists

A car moves. A population grows. A price changes.

Algebra can calculate totals and averages.

But the real question is:

What is happening right now?

Symbol

The Limit Notation

All the ideas above are written as:

lim (x → 2) f(x) = 4

✔ Symbol is just shorthand for what you already understand
Visual Trigonometry

Trigonometric Limit

As angle θ becomes very small:

sinθ ≈ θ when θ → 0

lim (θ → 0) sinθ / θ = 1

✔ This limit is the foundation of calculus
Motion → Slope

Derivative as Instantaneous Change

Slope of a curve at one point = derivative.

Derivative measures:

  • Speed from distance
  • Slope from curve

✔ Derivative = limit of average change
