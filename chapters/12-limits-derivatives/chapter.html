Concept 5

First Principles (Birth of Derivative)

Derivative comes from average change.

[f(x+h) − f(x)] / h

h becomes smaller and smaller

lim h→0
✔ All derivative formulas come from this
Derivative = lim h→0 [f(x+h) − f(x)] / h
