Sequences and Series

The mathematics of order and growth. Patterns, progressions, and infinite sums.

Why This Chapter Matters

Nature loves patterns. The petals of a flower, the breeding of rabbits, compound interest in banks—all follow specific mathematical sequences.

This chapter teaches us how to predict the future of a pattern. Whether it's linear growth (Arithmetic) or exponential growth (Geometric), understanding series is key to finance, computer science, and physics.

This chapter answers:
“What comes next, and what does it all add up to?”

What You Will Learn

Arithmetic Progression (A.P.)

Sequences with a constant difference.

Geometric Progression (G.P.)

Sequences with a constant ratio.

Means (A.M. & G.M.)

Inserting terms between numbers and their relationship.

Special Series

Sum of natural numbers, squares, and cubes.
