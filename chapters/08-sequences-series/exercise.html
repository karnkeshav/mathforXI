Subjective Questions

Q1. Determine the 20th term of the A.P. whose 7th term is 24 and 11th term is 34.

Show Answer
Solution: $a+6d=24$, $a+10d=34$. Subtracting: $4d=10 \implies d=2.5$.
$a + 15 = 24 \implies a=9$.
$a_{20} = 9 + 19(2.5) = 9 + 47.5 = 56.5$.

Q2. Find the sum of first 5 terms of a G.P. if $a=1$ and $r=2$.

Show Answer
Solution: $S_5 = \frac{1(2^5-1)}{2-1} = 31$.

Q3. Insert 3 numbers between 1 and 256 so that the resulting sequence is a G.P.

Show Answer
Solution: 1, $g_1, g_2, g_3$, 256. Total 5 terms.
$a_5 = ar^4 = 256 \implies 1 \cdot r^4 = 256 \implies r=4$.
Numbers: 4, 16, 64.

Q4. Find the sum to n terms of the series $1 \times 2 + 2 \times 3 + 3 \times 4 + \dots$

Show Answer
Solution: $T_k = k(k+1) = k^2+k$.
Sum = $\sum k^2 + \sum k = \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6} + \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$.
$= \frac{n(n+1)}{2} [\frac{2n+1}{3} + 1] = \frac{n(n+1)(n+2)}{3}$.
