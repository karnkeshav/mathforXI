Angle Measurement

[cite_start]

Degree Measure: 1 rotation = $360^\circ$[cite: 1978]. [cite_start]$1^\circ = 60'$ and $1' = 60''$[cite: 2827].

[cite_start]

Radian Measure: Angle subtended by arc length equal to radius[cite: 1982]. [cite_start]1 rotation = $2\pi$ rad[cite: 2885].

[cite_start]

Conversion: $\pi \text{ radians} = 180^\circ$[cite: 1990]. [cite_start]$1 \text{ rad} \approx 57^\circ 16'$[cite: 2917].

[cite_start]
$\text{Radian measure} = \frac{\pi}{180} \times \text{Degree measure}$ [cite: 2933]
