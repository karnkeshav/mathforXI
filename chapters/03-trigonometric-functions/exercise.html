Subjective Questions

Q1. Convert $40^\circ 20'$ into radian measure.

Show Answer
Solution: $40^\circ 20' = 40\frac{1}{3}^\circ = \frac{121}{3}^\circ$.
In radians: $\frac{121}{3} \times \frac{\pi}{180} = \frac{121\pi}{540}$ radians.

Q2. Find the value of $\sin \frac{31\pi}{3}$.

Show Answer
Solution: $\sin(10\pi + \frac{\pi}{3}) = \sin(\frac{\pi}{3}) = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$.

Q3. Prove that $\frac{\sin x - \sin 3x}{\sin^2 x - \cos^2 x} = 2\sin x$.

Show Answer
Solution: Numerator: $\sin x - \sin 3x = -(\sin 3x - \sin x) = -2\cos 2x \sin x$.
Denominator: $-(\cos^2 x - \sin^2 x) = -\cos 2x$.
Ratio: $\frac{-2\cos 2x \sin x}{-\cos 2x} = 2\sin x$.

Q4. Find the general solution of $\cos x = \frac{1}{2}$.

Show Answer
Solution: $\cos x = \cos \frac{\pi}{3}$.
General solution: $x = 2n\pi \pm \frac{\pi}{3}, n \in \mathbb{Z}$.
