Trigonometric Functions

Beyond triangles. Angles, waves, and the circular motion that drives the universe.

Why This Chapter Matters

You might know trigonometry as "finding heights and distances". But in Class XI, it evolves.

It becomes the study of rotation and periodic motion. Sound waves, light, AC current, and even the orbits of planets depend on Sine and Cosine functions.

This chapter bridges the gap between geometry and waves:
“How do we measure rotation and patterns that repeat forever?”
