Probability

The science of uncertainty. Experiments, outcomes, and the likelihood of events.

Why This Chapter Matters

Will it rain tomorrow? Will India win the match? Is this investment safe? Life is full of uncertainty. Probability is the mathematical tool we use to measure chance and make informed decisions.

This chapter builds on Sets and Permutations to formalise the study of random experiments, moving from "gut feeling" to precise numbers between 0 and 1.

This chapter answers:
“What are the odds?”

Events

Axiomatic Approach

Modern definition of probability: $P(E) = n(E)/n(S)$.
