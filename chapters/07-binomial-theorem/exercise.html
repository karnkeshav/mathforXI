Subjective Questions

Q1. Expand $(1-2x)^5$.

Show Answer
Solution: $^5C_0(1)^5 - ^5C_1(1)^4(2x) + ^5C_2(1)^3(2x)^2 - ^5C_3(1)^2(2x)^3 + ^5C_4(1)(2x)^4 - ^5C_5(2x)^5$.
$= 1 - 10x + 40x^2 - 80x^3 + 80x^4 - 32x^5$.

Q2. Find the coefficient of $x^6$ in $(x+2)^9$.

Show Answer
Solution: General term $T_{r+1} = ^9C_r x^{9-r} 2^r$.
For $x^6$, $9-r=6 \implies r=3$.
Coefficient: $^9C_3 \times 2^3 = \frac{9 \times 8 \times 7}{6} \times 8 = 84 \times 8 = 672$.

Q3. Find the middle term in the expansion of $(\frac{x}{3} + 9y)^{10}$.

Show Answer
Solution: $n=10$ (Even). Middle term is $T_6$.
$T_6 = ^{10}C_5 (\frac{x}{3})^5 (9y)^5 = 252 \times \frac{x^5}{243} \times 59049 y^5 = 61236 x^5 y^5$.

Q4. Which number is larger: $(1.1)^{10000}$ or 1000?

Show Answer
Solution: $(1.1)^{10000} = (1+0.1)^{10000} = 1 + 10000(0.1) + \dots = 1 + 1000 + \dots > 1001$.
So $(1.1)^{10000}$ is much larger.
