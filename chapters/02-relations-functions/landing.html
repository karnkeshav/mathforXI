Relations & Functions

Connecting two worlds. Inputs, outputs, and the rules that bind them.

Why This Chapter Matters

The entire universe works on cause and effect. You press a button, a light turns on. You throw a ball, it follows a path.

In mathematics, we call this connection a Function. It is the machine that transforms an input into an output.

This chapter answers:
“How do we mathematically describe relationships between quantities?”

What You Will Learn

Cartesian Product

Mixing two sets to create ordered pairs. The birthplace of coordinates.

Relations

Specific links between elements of two sets.

Functions

Special relations where every input has a unique output.

Domain & Range

Understanding what can go in and what comes out.
